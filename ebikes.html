Analog Motion eBikes

Lightweight, beautiful, and built for the city

AMX Black Edition

The highest-specification Analog Motion eBike with hydraulic disc brakes & Gates CDX belt drive. The Classic features straight handlebars for the most focussed riding position.

AMX Stealth

Sharing it's specification with the Limited Edition, the Stealth features zero branding and a matt black paint finish to fly under the radar.

AMX Limited Edition

The first Analog Motion to feature Gates Belt Drive, Schwalbe Kevlar tyres, & integrated lights. The Limited Edition (LE) was available in 2 colourways with a unique fork detail and was exclusively available during the 2020 AMX Indiegogo campaign.

AMX Mini

20" wheels & mini-velo inspired geometry. The Mini is ultra-compact and weighs sub-13kg!

AMX Road

An eBike with an aggressive riding position for those who want to dig in and pedal hard. Made to order. Less than 20 were ever manufactured.

AMX Capital/City

The core and original AMX. Signature colours, silver hardware, chain driven. The Capital and City launched the AMX for the 2020 AMX Indiegogo campaign.

AM1+

The AM1+ features the same geometry as the AM1 but is 30% more powerful. The AM1+ features Tektro mechanical disc brakes to ensure it stops as well as it goes. The deep-rimmed wheels are available in silver or black with matching hardware.

AM1

The AM1 is debut e-bike launched on Kickstarter in 2018. The progressive frame geometry is inspired by fixed gear track bikes. The 420 mm straight handlebars provide a responsive and agile feel. The deep-rimmed wheels create a clean aerodynamic aesthetic. Finished with silver hardware.
