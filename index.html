Posts from the Riders

Dear Riders,

We started Analog Motion in 2018 with a simple goal: to make beautiful, attainable eBikes. With no prior experience in the bike industry, we spent years hustling through evenings and weekends — building prototypes, establishing a supply chain, and shaping a dream.

In 2018, we launched on Kickstarter and went on to deliver nearly 3,000 bikes across 200 cities worldwide through multiple successful crowdfunding campaigns, even navigating Brexit and a global pandemic — earning praise for the simplicity and joy our bikes brought to riding.

Sadly, that dream has come to an end. Analog Motion closed in 2023. As hard as it was to accept, it became clear there was no viable future for the company. In the end, we ran out of time to secure the funding or opportunities needed to keep the business alive. To those who were affected by the closure, we are truly sorry.

To everyone who believed in us, rode our bikes, and shared in the journey — thank you. We would never have made it as far as we did without your support.

With gratitude,
Nav & Jack
